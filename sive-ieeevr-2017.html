New paper accepted into SIVE 2017!

My paper titled "An Approach to Reducing Distance Compression in Audiovisual Virtual Environments" has been accepted into the SIVE (Sonic Interactions in Virtual Environments) workshop as part of IEEE VR 2017! I will be presenting my paper and it will be published as part of the conference proceedings. See you in LA!

Reference

Finnegan, D. J., O'Neill, E., & Proulx, M. J.
An approach to reducing distance compression in audiovisual virtual environments.
In Sonic Interactions for Virtual Environments (SIVE), 2017 IEEE 3rd VR Workshop on (pp. 1-6). IEEE.
DOI: 10.1109/SIVE.2017.7901607
