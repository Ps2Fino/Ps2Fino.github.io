Invited talk at the Sherborne Science Cafe

Last night I gave a talk at The Sherborne Science Cafe in the beautiful town of Sherborne. It was the biggest audience I’ve spoken to so far (>> 50 people). The talk was the same talk I gave at this years Pint of Science with some tweaks from feedback after PoS itself. There were many great questions from the audience ranging from the purpose of VR, the ethics of VR and machine intelligence more generally, and of course, ‘When will we have the holodeck!?*’.

The science cafe have written a report of the night and put it up on their site which you can check out. For video links, check out my post on the Raven Science Cafe
