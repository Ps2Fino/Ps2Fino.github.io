Invited talk @ Pint of Science

I've been invited to talk at Bath's Pint of Science next Month. If you're in Bath and missed my previous talk at The Raven, then why not come and hear v2.0!? I'll be talking about the close relationship between psychology and the technology involved in virtual reality (VR), and some of my latest research on perception in VR. You can buy tickets for the event here.

Update

The night was a success!! Some great questions asked from a curious audience. The event was sold out, and I was delighted to take part this year. I very much hope to do so again next year.
